RESEARCH INTERNSHIP FUNDING MANAGEMENT PLATFORM
Ministry of Higher Education and Scientific Research - Algeria
Comprehensive Guide for Researchers and PhD Students
First Edition - Academic Year 2024/2025
 
================================================================================
SECTION 1: PLATFORM OVERVIEW
================================================================================
 
The Research Internship Funding Management Platform is a digital platform launched
by the Algerian Ministry of Higher Education and Scientific Research to simplify
and modernize the procedures for submitting funding requests for scientific
internships abroad and domestically. The platform allows researchers and PhD
students to submit their applications electronically and track their status at
all stages of processing.
 
Platform Objectives:
- Digitize funding application procedures for scientific internships
- Reduce file processing time from 3 months to 3 weeks
- Ensure transparency and equality in grant distribution
- Enable university institutions to follow up on their researchers files
- Archive and preserve all documents and correspondence electronically
 
================================================================================
SECTION 2: TYPES OF FUNDED INTERNSHIPS
================================================================================
 
1. Scientific Internships Abroad (Court Sejour)
Duration: 1 to 6 months
Target: PhD students and researcher professors wishing to conduct research
in specialized laboratories at partner foreign universities.
Grant includes: round trip flight ticket, accommodation, health insurance,
and a monthly grant of 1,200 euros.
 
2. Domestic Scientific Internships
Target: Researchers wishing to conduct internships in other national
laboratories outside their original institution.
Duration: 1 week to 2 months.
Covers: transportation and accommodation costs at a rate of 3,000
Algerian dinars per day.
 
3. Participation in International Conferences and Symposiums
Support for researchers participating in internationally ranked scientific
conferences. The researcher must have an accepted scientific paper at the
conference. The grant covers registration fees, travel and accommodation.
 
================================================================================
SECTION 3: ELIGIBILITY CONDITIONS
================================================================================
 
General Conditions for Applicants:
- Must be enrolled in a PhD program or hold the rank of lecturer or above
- Must not have benefited from the same type of grant in the last 2 years
- Must obtain approval from the original institution and scientific supervisor
- Must have an official invitation from the host institution (for abroad internships)
- Must be in good administrative standing
- Age must not exceed 45 years for abroad internships
 
Special Conditions for PhD Students:
- Must have completed at least the first year of PhD
- Must obtain a positive report from the PhD follow-up committee
- Must submit a clear research plan showing the benefit of the internship
 
================================================================================
SECTION 4: REQUIRED DOCUMENTS
================================================================================
 
Essential Administrative Documents:
1. Electronic application form filled via the platform
2. Copy of valid national identity card
3. Work certificate or PhD enrollment certificate
4. Academic CV in French or English
5. List of scientific publications if any
6. Approval from head of original institution, signed and stamped
7. Approval from scientific supervisor
 
Technical and Scientific Documents:
1. Research protocol to be executed during the internship
2. Official invitation from host laboratory or university
3. Detailed description of internship program and scientific content
4. Recommendation letter from laboratory director or research team
 
Additional Documents for Abroad Internships:
1. Valid passport (minimum 6 months validity after return date)
2. International health insurance document
3. Language proficiency certificate for host country language if required
 
================================================================================
SECTION 5: APPLICATION PROCEDURE
================================================================================
 
Step 1 - Create Account:
Visit the electronic platform and create your account using your institutional
email. You will receive an activation email within 24 hours.
 
Step 2 - Choose Application Type:
After logging in, choose the appropriate internship type (abroad, domestic,
or international conference) and verify that you meet the eligibility conditions.
 
Step 3 - Upload Documents:
Upload all required documents in PDF format. Maximum file size per document
is 5 megabytes. Ensure that scanned documents are clear and legible.
 
Step 4 - Review File:
Review your complete file before submission. After submission, documents
cannot be modified unless a correction is requested by the committee.
 
Step 5 - Follow Up:
You can track your file status in real time via your personal dashboard.
You will receive email notifications at every update to your file status.
 
================================================================================
SECTION 6: DEADLINES AND SESSIONS
================================================================================
 
Applications open four times per year according to the following schedule:
 
Session 1 (January):   Deadline January 31    - Results announced March 15    - Summer internships
Session 2 (April):     Deadline April 30       - Results announced June 15     - Autumn internships
Session 3 (July):      Deadline July 31        - Results announced September 15 - Winter internships
Session 4 (October):   Deadline October 31    - Results announced December 15  - Spring internships
 
Important note: Submitting a file after the specified deadline will not be
accepted under any circumstances. Study periods range from 2 to 3 weeks
from the date of closing applications.
 
================================================================================
SECTION 7: FUNDING AMOUNTS
================================================================================
 
Round trip flight ticket:         Up to 150,000 DZD (depending on destination)
Monthly grant (abroad):           1,200 euros per month
Daily accommodation allowance (domestic): 3,000 DZD per day
Conference registration fees:    Up to 50,000 DZD for the entire internship
Health insurance:                 Fully covered
 
================================================================================
SECTION 8: POST-INTERNSHIP OBLIGATIONS
================================================================================
 
The funding beneficiary must submit the final internship report within a
maximum of 30 days from the return date. The report must include:
 
- Summary of completed work and achieved objectives
- List of publications or scientific articles resulting from the internship
- Internship completion certificate signed by the host institution
- Copies of all financial invoices and receipts
- Return of unspent amounts if any
 
In case of failure to submit the final report on time, the Ministry has the
right to recover the granted amounts and exclude the researcher from
benefiting from any future funding for 3 years.
 
================================================================================
SECTION 9: FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
================================================================================
 
Q: Can I submit two applications at the same time?
A: No, you cannot submit more than one application in the same session.
You must wait for the result of the first application before submitting a new one.
 
Q: What happens if my file is rejected?
A: In case of rejection, you will receive a notification with the reasons,
and you have the right to reapply in the next session after correcting
the mentioned deficiencies.
 
Q: Can the internship duration be extended?
A: A maximum one-month extension can be requested for abroad internships,
provided scientific justifications and supervisor approval are submitted.
The extension request must be submitted at least 15 days before the
original end date.
 
Q: Does the funding cover family members?
A: No, the funding covers the researcher only, not family members.
 
Q: How is the grant paid?
A: The grant is paid via bank transfer directly to the researcher account.
You must provide the platform with your international bank account number
(IBAN) at least 2 weeks before the internship start date.
 
Q: What languages are accepted for file documents?
A: Documents are accepted in Arabic, French and English only.
Documents written in other languages must be translated and authenticated.
